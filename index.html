Key Point: SmartCal is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers.

1. Information We Don't Collect

SmartCal is a client-side application, which means:

  • We do not collect your personal information
  • We do not store your food logs on our servers
  • We do not track your usage patterns
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not share data with third parties

2. How Your Data is Stored

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SQLite Database: Your food logs, weight history, and settings
  • Local Storage: App preferences and API key (encrypted)
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't backup your data to any cloud service

3. Third-Party Services

OpenRouter.ai API:

  • You provide your own API key
  • Food/exercise queries are sent to OpenRouter.ai for AI processing
  • We don't store or log these requests
  • OpenRouter.ai's privacy policy applies to their service
  • You can review OpenRouter.ai's privacy policy at: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

4. Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Export all your data anytime
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • API Key: You manage your own OpenRouter.ai API key

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Security

Since all data stays on your device:

  • Your device's security features protect your data
  • API keys are stored securely in encrypted local storage
  • No data transmission to our servers means no server-side breaches

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates.
